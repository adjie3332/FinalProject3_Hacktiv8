About Us

Coding.in is a startup company that aims to develop a developer ecosystem in Indonesia. Founded on January 5, 2022, Coding.in has an electronic learning platform on the Coding-in.com page.

Coding.in's vision is to become a leading technology education platform that promotes greater access to digital literacy for all. Coding.in has a mission to accelerate Indonesia's transition to a digital world through technology education that transforms lives.

Now all nations are moving towards a digital world that is based on technological innovation in all walks of life. We believe that technology education is the foundation for every nation to be at the forefront of facing the digital world.

Coding.in exists as a technology education platform that helps produce global-standard digital talent. All for the sake of accelerating Indonesia to be at the forefront.
